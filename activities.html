Activities

Bora Bora is a paradise for adventure seekers and relaxation seekers alike. From water sports to land adventures to spa treatments, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Water Sports

Jet Skiing

Explore the stunning lagoon of Bora Bora at high speed on a jet ski. Feel the thrill of zipping across the azure water, taking in the spectacular views of the island and surrounding motus. Guided jet ski tours are available for both beginners and experienced riders.

Paddle Boarding

Enjoy the tranquil beauty of Bora Bora's lagoon by stand-up paddleboarding. This popular water sport is perfect for all skill levels and offers a fun and relaxing way to explore the lagoon while getting a full-body workout.

Land Adventures

Hiking

Explore the lush tropical landscape and stunning views of Bora Bora by hiking its numerous trails. From easy walks to challenging treks, there's a trail suited for every skill level. Discover hidden waterfalls, ancient ruins, and breathtaking vistas during your adventure.

ATV Tours

Experience the thrill of riding an all-terrain vehicle (ATV) through Bora Bora's diverse landscapes. Guided tours take you through rugged terrain, lush rainforests, and along sandy beaches. It's an exciting way to explore the island's hidden gems.

Relaxation

Spa Treatments

Indulge in luxurious spa treatments while surrounded by the natural beauty of Bora Bora. Choose from a variety of massages, body wraps, and facials that incorporate local ingredients and ancient Polynesian techniques to rejuvenate your body and mind.

Beach Yoga

Unwind and connect with nature by participating in beach yoga sessions. Practice various yoga poses on the soft sands of Bora Bora's pristine beaches, accompanied by the gentle sound of waves and the refreshing ocean breeze. Suitable for all skill levels, beach yoga offers a serene and rejuvenating experience.
