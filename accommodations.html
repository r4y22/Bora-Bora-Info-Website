Accommodations

Bora Bora has many different styles of accommodations, some are quite pricey, some may be affordable, but they are all beautiful in their own way!

Flight Ticket Prices

You can travel to Bora Bora in many different ways, travel costs usually average from around $3000+, but these prices are subject to change.

The most common way to travel to Bora Bora is by plane, but be aware, it is impossible to fly to Bora Bora non-stop, you will likely have to stop at one of the islands, and take a short flight from there as well to Bora Bora where you are usually greeted by Four Seasons.

Luxury Accommodations

Bora Bora is known for its luxury accommodations, and there are many different options to choose from. The most popular and luxurious resort is the Four Seasons, which is located on Motu Toopua, a private island in the lagoon. The Four Seasons is a 5-star resort, and is known for its beautiful overwater bungalows, and its amazing service. The Four Seasons is also known for its amazing spa, and its many different restaurants. The Four Seasons is a great place to stay if you are looking for a luxurious and relaxing vacation. Prices usually range from $4000 - $5000 per night, and can go up to $30,000 a night

Budget Friendly Accommodations

If you are looking for a more budget-friendly option, there are many different options to choose from. The most popular and budget-friendly resort is the Intercontinental Resort, which is located on Motu Piti Aau, a private island in the lagoon. The Intercontinental Resort is a 4-star resort, and is known for its beautiful overwater bungalows, and its amazing service. Prices usually range from $2000+ per night, and can go up to $7000 a night

Super Budget Friendly Accommodations

If you are looking for a super budget-friendly option, there are many different options to choose from. The most popular and super budget-friendly hotel is the Hotel Royal Bora Bora, it is located on the beach, and it is a 3.5 star hotel. Prices usually range from $300+ per night, and can go up to $400 a night
